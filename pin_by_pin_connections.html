MT3608 Module Onboard potentiometer (pot)
→
Adjust with small screwdriver Set VOUT = exactly 5.0V
Turn pot slowly, measure with multimeter at OUT+ and OUT− until reading is 5.0V
⚠️ Critical step — ALWAYS do this before connecting ESP32:
1. Connect MT3608 input to battery (via TP4056 OUT+)
2. Connect multimeter probes to MT3608 OUT+ and OUT−
3. Turn the small potentiometer screw slowly until meter reads 5.00V
4. Only then connect the output to ESP32 VIN
Over-voltage (above 5.5V) will permanently damage the ESP32.
